Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What we collect

When you submit the contact form on this website, we collect your name and email address. This is handled through Formspree, a third-party form service.

We use website analytics to understand how visitors use this site. The analytics we use are privacy-first and do not place cookies on your device.

How we use your data

Your name and email are used only to respond to your inquiry. We do not use them for marketing without your explicit consent.

What we do not do

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with third parties except Formspree, which processes form submissions on our behalf.

Your rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us using the contact form.

Governing law

This policy is governed by Belgian law and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

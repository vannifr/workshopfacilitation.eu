Case studies

Global product team: from deadlock to delivery

The situation

A global product team across multiple time zones was stuck. Virtual meetings were dominated by a few voices. Others had disengaged. Deadlines were slipping and stakeholder trust was eroding.

What we did

  • Diagnosed the real blockers through structured pre-work
  • Ran a series of virtual workshops using Liberating Structures for balanced participation
  • Built shared decision-making frameworks and meeting protocols
  • Trained the team in self-facilitation so they wouldn't need us indefinitely

Results

  • Meeting time reduced — same output in less time
  • Decision cycles shortened from weeks to days
  • Team satisfaction scores increased
  • On-time delivery improved

"The transformation in our team dynamics has been remarkable. We now accomplish more in shorter meetings, with everyone actively contributing."

— VP of Product

Post-merger leadership alignment across cultures

The situation

Following a merger, a leadership team from several European countries struggled to work together. Different cultural approaches to feedback, decision-making, and planning were creating tension and slowing everything down.

What we did

  • Designed a multi-day offsite with cultural mapping exercises
  • Used visual harvesting to make the vision alignment visible
  • Ran LEGO® Serious Play® sessions to surface unspoken assumptions about leadership
  • Built explicit team norms and communication agreements

Results

  • Unified multi-year vision with clear role alignment
  • Fewer cultural misunderstandings in day-to-day work
  • Communication protocols that the team actually used
  • Improved psychological safety scores

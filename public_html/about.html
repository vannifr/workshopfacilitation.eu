Case studies

Global product team: from conflict to synergy

The situation

A global product team across multiple time zones was stuck. Virtual meetings were dominated by a few voices. Others had disengaged. Deadlines were slipping and stakeholder trust was eroding.

What we did

  • Assessed communication patterns and diagnosed the real blockers
  • Ran a series of virtual workshops using Liberating Structures for balanced participation
  • Developed shared decision-making frameworks and meeting protocols
  • Trained the team in self-facilitation techniques

Results

  • Meeting time reduced significantly, with higher productivity
  • Decision cycles shortened substantially
  • Team satisfaction scores increased markedly
  • On-time delivery improved dramatically

"The transformation in our team dynamics has been remarkable. We now accomplish more in shorter meetings, with everyone actively contributing."

— VP of Product

Post-merger leadership alignment across cultures

The situation

Following a merger, a leadership team from several European countries struggled to integrate. Cultural approaches to feedback, decision-making, and planning were creating tension and slowing everything down.

What we did

  • Designed a multi-day offsite with cultural mapping exercises
  • Used visual facilitation for vision alignment
  • Ran LEGO® Serious Play® sessions to explore leadership challenges
  • Developed explicit team norms and communication agreements

Results

  • Unified multi-year vision with clear role alignment
  • Substantial reduction in cultural misunderstandings
  • Communication protocols that respected diversity
  • Improved psychological safety scores

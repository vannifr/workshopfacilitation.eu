Get better at facilitation

You already run meetings and workshops. You know some work and some don't. The difference is usually structure -- having the right method for the right situation, and knowing how to read the room when things go sideways. That's what we teach.

What you'll learn

Liberating Structures

33+ microstructures for group engagement. Start with 1-2-4-All in your next meeting. Within a month, you'll have a dozen techniques you use without thinking.

Learn more

Visual Facilitation

Capture ideas visually during sessions. You don't need to be an artist -- you need a few techniques for making group thinking visible and memorable.

LEGO Serious Play

Use 3D model-building to surface insights that words can't reach. Especially powerful for strategy, team identity, and complex problem-solving.

Learn more

Consent Decision Making

Move beyond consensus (everyone agrees) and voting (someone loses). Consent-based decisions are faster, better, and stick.

Learn more

Three formats

Workshop (1-2 days)

Intensive, hands-on training in a specific method. You practice on real scenarios, not case studies. Walk out ready to use it.

Best for: Getting started with a new method quickly

Practitioner Program (3-6 months)

Learn, practice, debrief, repeat. Training sessions alternated with real-world application and coaching. This is how facilitation skills actually stick.

  • Training sessions
  • Practice with coaching
  • Custom playbook for your context
  • Mentoring between sessions
  • Community of practice

Best for: Building deep, lasting capability

Coaching & Mentoring

Already facilitating but want to get sharper? We observe your sessions, give honest feedback, and help you design better ones.

Best for: Experienced facilitators who want a sparring partner

How we work with you

Understand

We talk to key people. We learn what's really going on -- not just what's on the meeting agenda.

Design

We pick the right methods for your situation -- not a one-size template. Every session is built from scratch.

Facilitate

We run the session. Everyone participates. Nobody hides. The structure does the heavy lifting.

Capture

Decisions, actions, and owners -- documented before we leave. No ambiguity about what was agreed.

Transfer

Your team walks away knowing how to do this without us. We teach the methods, not just use them.

Two ways to work together

Single Session

You have a specific challenge. A decision that won't make itself, a team that can't align, a conversation that keeps getting postponed. We design a session around it, facilitate it, and make sure you leave with something concrete.

Sessions run 2–8 hours. We'll talk before to understand what you actually need — not just what's on the agenda. A week or two from first conversation to facilitated session is typical.

  • Product roadmap prioritisation
  • Strategic planning offsite
  • Team charter or team reset
  • Quarterly retrospective

Multi-Session Program

Some challenges don't resolve in a day. A team that keeps having the same conflict. A leadership group that agrees in meetings and pulls in opposite directions the rest of the time. A merger that is technically complete but not culturally.

A 2–3 month program gives us enough time to work with what is actually happening — not just the presenting problem. Sessions spaced across weeks, with skill transfer built in so the team doesn't need us indefinitely.

  • Leadership team alignment
  • Cross-cultural integration
  • Agile ways of working

In-person, virtual, or hybrid. We facilitate in English, Dutch, and French.

Common questions

How far in advance should we book?

Two to three weeks is typical. For complex programs, four to six weeks. If it's urgent, call us -- we've designed sessions in under a week when needed.

Do you work remotely?

Yes. About half our sessions are virtual. We use the same structured methods online -- they work just as well when facilitated properly.

What if we're not sure what we need?

That's what the discovery call is for. We'll ask questions, listen, and tell you honestly whether facilitation is the right tool for your situation.

What does it cost?

Every engagement is different -- group size, preparation, format, and duration all factor in. We'll give you a clear, no-surprise quote after the discovery call.

What makes you different from other facilitators?

Two things: we specialize in multicultural and cross-functional teams, and we use structured methods (like Liberating Structures) that guarantee participation from everyone -- not just the confident speakers.

Ready to have a conversation?

No pitch. No pressure. A 30-minute call to understand your challenge and see if we can help.
